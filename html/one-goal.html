One Goal


"One Goal: A Coach, a Team, and the Game That Brought a Divided Town Together" is a book by Amy Bass that tells the inspiring story of a high school soccer team and their coach in Lewiston, Maine. The team is composed of a diverse group of Somali refugees who have settled in the town, and their journey to the state championship serves as a unifying force in a community facing challenges of integration and cultural differences. The book highlights how sports can bridge divides, create bonds, and bring people together, showcasing the transformative power of soccer in overcoming obstacles and fostering unity.
