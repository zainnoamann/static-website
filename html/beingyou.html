Being You


"Being You: A New Science of Consciousness" is a 2021 non-fiction book by neuroscientist Anil Seth, published by Faber and Faber. The book delves into Seth's theory of consciousness and self, centered around three core components: conscious level, conscious content, and conscious self. Seth's childhood curiosity about consciousness led to his multidisciplinary team at Sussex collaborating on the book, aiming to provide a satisfying scientific explanation of conscious experience. The book weaves together various philosophical and scientific perspectives, supported by personal anecdotes, and took three years to write with the support of a Wellcome Trust Engagement Fellowship.
