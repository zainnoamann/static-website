Wayne Rooney My Decade In the Premier League


"Wayne Rooney: My Decade in the Premier League" is an autobiography written by English former professional footballer Wayne Rooney. In this book, Rooney reflects on his experiences and accomplishments during his time in the English Premier League, one of the most competitive and popular football leagues in the world. The autobiography provides insights into his career, his role as a key player for Manchester United, and his contributions to the sport. Rooney's personal journey, achievements, and challenges are explored within the context of his significant impact on the Premier League and the broader football landscape.
