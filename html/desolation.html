A Desolation Called Peace


"A Desolation Called Peace" is an enthralling science fiction novel by Arkady Martine, serving as a riveting sequel to "A Memory Called Empire." Ambassador Mahit Dzmare finds herself embroiled in the delicate realm of diplomacy within the Teixcalaanli Empire and its neighboring systems. As tensions escalate and hidden forces threaten stability, Mahit must navigate a web of political intrigue while grappling with her own identity and the allure of a foreign culture. With masterful storytelling and thought-provoking themes, the novel explores power, assimilation, and the price of maintaining peace in a captivating galaxy on the verge of chaos.
