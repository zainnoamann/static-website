The Uniquet Ones


"The Unquiet Ones: A History of Pakistan Cricket" is a book penned by Pakistani sports journalist Osman Samiuddin. Published by HarperCollins India, the book delves into the transformation of cricket in Pakistan, evolving from an exclusive urban sport into a unifying force across the diverse landscape of the country. Divided into five sections comprising thirty-one essays, each section chronicles distinct eras in Pakistan cricket, starting with defining matches and featuring key players. The book's comprehensive narrative spans over decades, culminating in the last 22 years up to early 2014, offering insights into the dynamic journey of Pakistan's cricket history."
