Privacy Policy


At Whispering Pages Bookstore, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding your personal information.


Information We Collect


When you interact with our website or visit our physical store, we may collect certain information to provide you with
        the best possible service. The types of data we collect include: 
        Personal Information: This may include your name, email address, phone number, postal address, and other contact
        details.
        Payment Information: When you make a purchase, we collect payment details such as credit card information. Please rest
        assured that we use secure and encrypted payment methods to protect your financial data.
        Website Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use our website, including your browsing activities, pages
        visited, and interactions with our website features.

How We Use Your Information


We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:
        Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders, manage payments, and deliver products to your preferred address.
        Customer Support:
        To assist you with inquiries, provide customer support, and address any issues related to your
        purchases.
        Communication:
        To keep you informed about new releases, special offers, promotions, and updates related to Whispering
        Pages Bookstore. You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
        Improving User Experience:
        To enhance our website, product offerings, and services based on your feedback and browsing
        patterns.

Data Protection and Security


We prioritize the security of your personal information and have implemented appropriate measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We use secure servers and follow industry-standard security protocols to ensure the safety of your data.


Third-Party Disclosure


We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except for trusted third parties involved in the operation of our website, delivery services, or payment processing. These partners are obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the intended purpose.


Your Rights


You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information in our records. If you have any questions, concerns, or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us using the details provided at the end of this policy.


Cookies


Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and track website usage. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.


Updates to Privacy Policy


We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be indicated at the top.
