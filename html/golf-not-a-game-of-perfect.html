Golf Is Not A Game Of Perfect


"Golf Is Not a Game of Perfect" by Dr. Bob Rotella is a valuable guide for golfers seeking to improve their mental approach to the game. Focusing on the psychological aspects of golf, the book offers practical advice and strategies to handle challenges like pressure and self-doubt. Dr. Rotella emphasizes the significance of confidence, positive thinking, and staying present in the moment. The book is a renowned resource for golfers of all levels looking to enhance their mental game and overall performance on the golf course.
