The Hollow Places


"The Hollow Places" by T. Kingfisher is a chilling horror novel that follows Kara Harper as she stumbles upon a mysterious hole in the wall of her uncle's antique shop. Venturing into this enigmatic portal, she finds herself in a nightmarish realm filled with otherworldly terrors and dark secrets. Teaming up with Simon, a quirky museum owner, they must confront ancient malevolent forces and navigate a haunting landscape where reality and nightmares collide. With its eerie atmosphere and captivating storytelling, "The Hollow Places" delivers a spine-tingling and unforgettable journey into the unknown.
