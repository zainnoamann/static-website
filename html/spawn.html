Spawn


In "Spawn" by Shaun Huston, Harold Pierce, haunted by a tragic childhood accident, spends decades in a mental asylum until his release to work as a porter in a hospital. Meanwhile, a killer named Paul Harvey escapes, and the police fear he will return to his hometown. As Pierce struggles with his sanity, his job involves burning foetuses, triggering dark memories. He decides to bury them instead, but a shocking discovery reveals three foetuses still alive. As the police hunt Harvey, Pierce's mental state deteriorates, leading to a shocking series of events involving murders and an unsettling revelation about the foetuses' sinister power. The story delves into the depths of human darkness and the horrors that lurk in the shadows.
