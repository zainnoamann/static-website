12 Rules for Life


"12 Rules for Life: An Antidote to Chaos" by Jordan B. Peterson is a self-help book offering twelve practical and philosophical principles to guide individuals in navigating life's challenges. Drawing from psychology, philosophy, mythology, and religion, the book presents rules that cover topics such as personal responsibility, meaning, discipline, and relationships. Through discussions and anecdotes, Peterson explores the intersection of practical advice and profound insights, sparking conversations about personal development and the human experience. The book has garnered both praise and controversy, making it a notable addition to the self-help genre.
