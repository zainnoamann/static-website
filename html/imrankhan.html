Imran Khan


Discover the captivating journey of Imran Khan, the former Pakistan cricket captain, and all-rounder. From cricket triumphs to political significance, Imran's life is filled with diverse facets: an Oxbridge graduate, vociferous campaigner, and devout Muslim with a colorful social life. Controversial and admired, he raised $60 million for cancer research. Acclaimed biographer Christopher Sandford unveils the enigmatic man through interviews with a varied cast of associates, providing exclusive access to Imran's inner sanctum.
