Nine Lives Of Pakistan


In "The Nine Lives of Pakistan: Dispatches from a Precarious State," author Declan Walsh reflects on his nine years in Pakistan and explores the factors that led to his expulsion. Through a series of chapters centered around influential personalities, including political leaders, military rulers, lawyers, and policemen, Walsh presents a compelling historical, political, and sociological analysis of post-9/11 Pakistan. Instead of focusing solely on historical events, he examines the lives and work of these individuals to shed light on the complex fabric of Pakistani society and its impact on the country's current political and social landscape.
