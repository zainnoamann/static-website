Sea Of Tranquility


"Sea of Tranquility" is a captivating novel by Emily St. John Mandel that seamlessly weaves art, time travel, love, and the devastation of a pandemic. From 1912 Vancouver Island to a distant moon colony five centuries later, the story follows Edwin St. Andrew, a young man exiled for a passionate outburst, and Olive Llewellyn, a renowned writer traversing Earth and her home on the moon. Through the lens of detective Gaspery-Jacques Roberts in Night City, their lives intersect, revealing extraordinary connections that challenge the fabric of time and space. With an intellectual playfulness and emotional depth, this mesmerizing tale brilliantly captures the essence of our present moment, leaving readers enchanted by its exploration of humanity across centuries and beyond.
